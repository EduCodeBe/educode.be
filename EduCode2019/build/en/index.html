Bienvenue à EduCode

Bruxelles, Belgique
27, 28, 29 août 2018

Bozar, ESI & ISIB & BeCentral
Académie royale

An international conference to inform, train and engage the interest of all education stakeholders in the digital challenges.

À propos d'EduCode

Educode is the first edition of an annual international symposium dedicated to education, practice and research in the areas related to digital. It is the result of collaboration between different key digital players in Belgium and abroad.

The Educode symposium will take place from 27 to 29 August 2018 on different sites located in Brussels within 500 meters of the Royal Palace and the Federal Parliament.

The organization of this conference is essentially voluntary. All help and support are welcome.
