< About EduCode />

27, 28 and 29 August 2018 in Brussels

EduCode is the first edition of an annual international symposium dedicated to digital education, practice and research. It is the result of collaboration between different key digital players in Belgium.

Digital technology is at the heart of transformations in the global society that are imposing on everyone. This is a huge challenge for our societies and we have no choice but to inform ourselves and train ourselves to respond to it. These changes inevitably involve the transformation of the school, which can only be gradual and framed, but no less determined and energetically conducted.

This induces a new relationship to knowledge, which itself also has profound effects on society. In fact, knowledge is no longer only in books or brains that are difficult to access. It appears as immediately at your fingertips after searching on the mobile phone connected to the internet. It is often more accurate and up to date than the one we have in mind. In short, the ubiquitous digital induces profound changes in our lives that must at least be identified in order to hope to understand and use them wisely or criticize them.

In the age of ubiquitous digital, teachers should be able to use and integrate digital technologies into their courses. Far from us the idea of ​​replacing the notebooks, pencils and pens, essential for a good training of the capacities of writing, synthesis and transposition. But it is important for children and learners in general, like their teachers, to be comfortable with digital technologies. That they can use the resources of their computers and mobile phones, that they can search with them, complete their skills, create, invent. And that they can reflect on issues, challenges, opportunities as much as limits and threats, and integrate these tools into their lives to better participate in the Society.

This involves a specific education, training, an opening to digital that the conference wants to bring.

The Educode.be colloquium will take place from 27 to 29 August 2018 on various sites located in Brussels.

Overview of the program

Monday 27

On August 27, 2018 in Bozar, rue Ravenstein, teachers, directorates and the general public will be able to attend lectures, a round table, reflection workshops and posters during the breaks to understand this new world, its challenges and opportunities. The lectures are open to the general public. The day ends with a symphonic concert with choirs.

More info

Thuesday 28

Tuesday, August 28 at the ESI, Graduate School of Computer Science and ISIB, Graduate School of Engineers of Brussels, rue Royale 67 and 150 next to the column of the Congress, teachers will be invited to 60 to 100 practical workshops during which they will be the learners, so as to find their alter-egos, develop with them content and experiences to reuse in class what they have learned. These hands-on workshops will focus on a wide variety of topics around education and the use of digital and computer thinking.

More info

Wednesday 29

Wednesday, August 29 at the Royal Academy, rue Ducale, those who think on the uses and seek to generalize them as much as to particularize them, researchers in education on the uses of the digital, will find themselves in a conference for exchanges of experiences and research presentations.
